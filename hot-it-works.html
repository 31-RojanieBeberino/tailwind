How Does It Work?

Your home takes you away

Step 1 Create Your Listing

1.Visit the Tailwind Trades website: Go to the Tailwind Trades website and sign in to your account if you have one. If you're a new user, sign up for an account (free ones available with limited ability to see listing). If you want to become a member you would sign up, undergo professional and home vetting and choose subscription plan.

2.Start your listing: Click on the "Create Listing" button to begin the process.

3. Introduce Yourself: Provide a brief introduction about yourself, your family, and any other potential co-hosts. Highlight your interests and hobbies, and mention your favorite travel destinations.

4.Promote Your Home with Pictures: Upload high-quality pictures of your home. Showcase different rooms, amenities, and any unique features that make your home stand out.

5.Calendar Availability: Use the calendar tool to specify when your home is available for exchanges. Make sure to keep it up-to-date.

Step 2 Send Exchange Requests

1.Explore Destinations: Use the search function on Tailwind Trades to find homes in destinations that interest you. Browse through available listings.

2.Send Personalized Messages: When you find a home you're interested in, send a personalized message to the host introducing yourself, explaining your interest in their home, and proposing an exchange.

3.Respond to Requests: Monitor your inbox for messages from other members who are interested in your home. Respond to their messages promptly.

4.Communicate through Messaging: Use the Tailwind Trades messaging system to discuss the details of your exchange with the host. This includes travel dates, expectations, and any special requirements.

Step 3 Become A Member And Finalize Your Exchange

1.Pre-Approval by both hosts: Once you and your counterpart have agreed on the terms and details of the exchange, the each host must pre-approve the exchange request. This indicates their willingness to proceed.

2.Membership: If you are not already a Member, sign up for a Tailwind Trades Membership. This membership costs $220 for a year and allows you to enjoy unlimited exchanges.

3.Finalize the Exchange: Once the exchange is pre-approved by the host and you have a Tailwind Trades Membership, you can finalize the exchange. Confirm the exchange dates and any additional details.

4.Reciprocal Exchange: Remember that Tailwind Trades encourages reciprocal exchanges, where two pilots exchange their homes, whether simultaneously or on different dates. This ensures a fair and balanced exchange.

5.Enjoy Your Trip: Prepare for your exchange, travel to your chosen destination, and enjoy your vacation in the comfort of a fellow member's home.

By following these steps, you'll be well on your way to listing your home and arranging exciting home exchanges with other Tailwind Trades members. Happy traveling!
